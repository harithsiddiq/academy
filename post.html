Unsure between PMP, CAPM, PRINCE2, or Agile certifications? This guide compares each option so you can make a confident decision.

Consider Your Experience

PMP is best suited for professionals with significant project experience, whereas CAPM is designed for beginners who want to learn the fundamentals.

Industry and Geography

PRINCE2 is widely adopted in the UK and Europe, while PMP is globally recognized. Agile certifications like PMI-ACP and CSM focus on flexible delivery methods.

Tip: If you’re early in your career, start with CAPM or a Scrum certification to build momentum and then move to PMP.

Costs and Time

Factor in training, exam fees, and required contact hours. Many learners opt for instructor-led bootcamps to accelerate preparation.
